The roast was so good that people forgot we were demonstrating design skills. Every critic persona we built started writing zingers instead of analysis. The Performer kept generating slop that was accidentally funnier than the critiques.

So we pulled it back. Made the system honest. Each persona attacks the design from their real discipline — marketing, UX, engineering, brand — and the Performer has to actually fix the problems, not just acknowledge them.

The result is 9 variations that look nothing alike but share one thing: none of them are slop. Every choice is intentional. Every pixel has a reason.
