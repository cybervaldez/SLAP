The Finding

Design can't save bad content.

BUT →

BRUTALIST → exposes the slop
WARM ORGANIC → hides it in comfort
ART DECO → raises expectations
design = lens, not bandage ★★
this changes everything

the frame matters as much as the picture
✨
Confession

We almost ruined it by being too funny.

The slapstick concept was supposed to prove that bad content is bad content no matter what you dress it in. But somewhere around draft three, we started genuinely enjoying the jokes. The comedy became the point instead of the evidence.

We caught ourselves. Barely. The humor stays — but it serves the argument now, not the other way around. Every punchline earns its place by making the finding clearer.

yes we know this is ironic

a design study about self-awareness that almost lacked self-awareness
