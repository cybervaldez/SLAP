CONFESSION
WE ALMOST RUINED IT BY BEING TOO FUNNY.
We started this project to prove a point about AI-generated design. Somewhere along the way, we got addicted to the comedy. The slapstick rewrites. The absurd personas. We had a mascot. We had catchphrases. We had a running gag about a sentient loading spinner. It was hilarious. It was also becoming exactly the kind of self-indulgent noise we were supposed to be criticizing.
"The irony of almost producing slop about slop was not lost on us."
So we killed the darlings. Cut the mascot. Stripped the tone back. What you're reading now is what survived.
