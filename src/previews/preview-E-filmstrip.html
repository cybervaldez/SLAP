Behind the Scenes: Director's Commentary

We almost turned this into a comedy special.


The roast was so sharp that people were laughing instead of learning. We had to find the balance: funny enough to engage, structured enough to teach.


Every persona had a voice. Every design variation had an opinion. We ran 9 costumes on the same script and watched 5 critics react to each one. The data told a story we didn't expect: the worse the content, the more the design mattered.


This was never about dunking on AI-generated text. It was about asking: what does deliberate design actually do?


— The Director
