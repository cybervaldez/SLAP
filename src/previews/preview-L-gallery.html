The Problem

Every AI page looks the same.

Purple gradients. Inter font. Rounded pill buttons. A hero section that says "Revolutionize Your Workflow." Technically correct, aesthetically generic, and emotionally bankrupt. Not because it's broken. Because it could belong to anyone.

The Tension

Design is a lens, not a bandage.

  • Brutalist exposes. Every flaw in the copy is visible. Nothing hides behind polish.
  • Warm Organic hides. Soft edges and earthy tones make mediocre copy feel intentional.
  • Art Deco elevates. The same words feel authoritative when dressed in gold and geometry.
