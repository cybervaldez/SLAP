Implementation Spec

  • Tab is a sibling of the peek layer (not a child) — avoids overflow: hidden clipping
  • Tab positioned at right: 120px; transform: translateX(-100%) — sits flush against the peek's left edge
  • Peek opens to 120px with box-shadow: -8px 0 24px rgba(0,0,0,0.35) for depth
  • On wipe: tab's right transitions to 100% (follows the expanding edge) and fades out
  • Arrow: simple ↓ with slide loop animation (1.8s cycle)
  • Click triggers same behavior as scroll-down past threshold
  • Tab appears after a 2s delay (auto-peek opens first, then tab fades in)
