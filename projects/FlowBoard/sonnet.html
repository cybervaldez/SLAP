Features

Everything your team needs.
Nothing you don't.

Purpose-built tools that keep projects moving and teams aligned — without the cognitive overhead.

Kanban & List Views

Visualize work exactly how your team thinks. Switch between kanban boards, lists, or timelines in one click — no migration required.

AI Task Routing

Smart automation analyzes each task and routes it to the right person based on workload, skills, and priority — so nothing slips through.

Timeline & Milestones

Plan sprints and quarters with drag-and-drop timelines. Spot bottlenecks before they happen and keep stakeholders always in the loop.

Threaded Comments

Keep context where work happens. Threaded discussions, @mentions, and emoji reactions so decisions never get lost in Slack threads.

Live Reporting

Auto-generated velocity charts, burn-down graphs, and custom dashboards. Share reports with stakeholders in a single shareable link.

100+ Integrations

Connect GitHub, Figma, Slack, Google Drive, Jira, and more. FlowBoard becomes the hub your team already works from — just better.
